DOPPIO GUSTO


If you're looking for a place where you can take a journey through the flavors of typical Italian cuisine,
you've come to the right place. At Doppio Gusto we offer the best recipes of Mediterranean cuisine,
respecting the tradition of Italy and Salento. We prepare our dishes with products of the highest
quality, favoring ingredients of Italian origin to preserve the flavors and aromas of Italian tradition.

OUR PASSION FOR QUALITY FOOD

In the Doppio Gusto restaurant you will find the real Italian cuisine and tradition, with a selection of typical specialties from Salento.
Here you will find gnocchi, ravioli and fresh handmade pasta made from authentic traditional recipes. Our special water grill allows
us to prepare delicious and tender grilled meats. Pizza is one of our most popular specialities: it's made from a flour mix that makes it
incredibly tasty and easily digestible, and baked in our exceptional gas & wood pizza oven.
